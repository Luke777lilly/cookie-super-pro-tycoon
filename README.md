# ⚡ COOKIE SUPER PRO TYCOON ⚡

**A high-octane incremental clicker game with a boxing theme!**

🍪 Click cookies → 🥊 Build a boxing empire → 🏆 Become a champion!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

### **Manual Clicking**
Click the massive glowing cookie to earn cookies instantly. Each click = 1 cookie.

### **Buy Upgrades**
Use your cookies to purchase upgrades that generate passive income (cookies per second):

| Upgrade | Cost | CPS | Description |
|---------|------|-----|-------------|
| 🥊 **Pro Boxer** | 50 | 1 | Your first fighter joins the team |
| 👑 **Heavyweight Champ** | 300 | 10 | A world-class champion |
| 🏟️ **Boxing Stadium** | 1,500 | 50 | Host massive boxing events |
| 🏆 **WBA Title Belt** | 10,000 | 250 | The ultimate championship trophy |
| 🌌 **Cosmic Gym** | 50,000 | 1,000 | Train fighters across the multiverse |

### **Strategy**
- Each upgrade gets **more expensive** after you buy it (cost scales by 1.5x to 2.2x)
- Buy upgrades that maximize your **Cookies Per Second (CPS)**
- Higher CPS = faster progression to expensive upgrades
- Plan ahead: sometimes it's worth waiting to buy a better upgrade!

## 📝 Tips & Tricks

💡 **Early Game:**
- Click the cookie rapidly to build up your first 50 cookies
- Buy your first Pro Boxer ASAP to start passive income

💡 **Mid Game:**
- Focus on buying upgrades in order (they get exponentially more efficient)
- Your CPS should be continuously growing

💡 **Late Game:**
- Stack upgrades to reach astronomical cookie counts
- The Cosmic Gym is the ultimate passive income machine
